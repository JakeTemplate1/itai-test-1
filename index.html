Keeping Democracy Real

03 — Identity Fraud

Your Face Is No Longer Enough

AI-generated profile photos are now standard in scams and fake accounts. One person can create thousands of believable identities at once — including ones that slip past traditional identity checks.

Banks, apps, and social platforms now rely on AI face detection as a must-have first line of defense.

⬡ Asset Needed — Personal Harm (Keep Non-Graphic)
What works: Abstract and metaphorical only — a cracked phone screen with a padlock, a silhouette with a red "DETECTED · SYNTHETIC" badge overlay, or a closed laptop with a warning icon. Protecting Real People

Fabricated images used for blackmail have become a real, documented problem — with some categories up over 6,000% in early 2025. Laws like the U.S. TAKE IT DOWN Act are helping, but victims need fast proof that something is fake.

That's where we come in. IsThisAI gives victims, platforms, and law enforcement the evidentiary baseline they need to act.

05 — Brand & Reputation Risk

A Growing Issue for Everyone

Fake executive videos, cloned voices, or AI-generated posts can damage reputations and spread misinformation in minutes. Creators, small businesses, and individuals are increasingly targeted by impersonation and brand manipulation.
