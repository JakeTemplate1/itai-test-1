What we detect

One tool. Three detection engines.

Whether it's an image, a video clip, or a voice recording — get a verdict in seconds.

Image Detection

Pixel-level forensics

GAN fingerprints, diffusion artifacts, facial geometry flaws — caught at the signal level.

  • Identifies the exact generator — Midjourney, DALL·E, Flux, Firefly, and 70+ more
  • Heatmap highlights every region that triggered the detection
  • Detects partial edits, inpainting, and generative fill on otherwise real photos

JPG · PNG · WEBP · HEIC · GIF · URL

Video Detection

Frame-by-frame analysis

Single-frame detectors miss what happens between frames. Ours analyzes the whole sequence.

  • Catches temporal inconsistencies, synthetic motion, and edge blending errors
  • Detects face swaps, AI-generated scenes, and voice-synced lip dubs
  • Works on clips from any source — social downloads, direct upload, or URL

MP4 · MOV · WEBM · AVI · URL

Audio Detection

Spectral voice analysis

Neural synthesis leaves patterns in the spectrogram invisible to the ear — not to our models.

  • Identifies ElevenLabs, Suno, Udio, Play.ht, Murf, and 15+ voice generators
  • Works on voice clips, AI music, podcast dubbing, and synthetic soundscapes
  • Flags specific frequency bands where synthesis artifacts cluster

MP3 · WAV · M4A · FLAC · OGG

  • Specific generator identification — not just "AI vs. human"
